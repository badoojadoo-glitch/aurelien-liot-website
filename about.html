Aurelien Liot: Bridging Dublin and Manhattan.

Originally from Ireland and with solid legal experience in New York, Aurelien Liot established his practice with a clear mission: to simplify legal complexity for his clients.

A recognized member of the legal community, he combines the warmth of an Irish welcome with the efficiency of New York State law. The firm specializes in assisting individuals and businesses with transatlantic interests, ensuring that every signed document holds indisputable legal validity on both sides of the ocean.
